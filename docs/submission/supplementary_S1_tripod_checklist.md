# Supplementary File S1 — TRIPOD 2015 Checklist

**Manuscript:** Beyond Binary Lock: A Continuous Aperture-Collapse Hazard Model of Middle-Ear Barotrauma in Hypobaric-Chamber Training

**Authors:** Malpica DL, Farfán MA.

**Reporting guideline:** Collins GS, Reitsma JB, Altman DG, Moons KGM. Transparent Reporting of a multivariable prediction model for Individual Prognosis Or Diagnosis (TRIPOD): The TRIPOD Statement. *Ann Intern Med.* 2015;162(1):55–63. doi:10.7326/M14-0697.

**Study type (TRIPOD Type):** **Type 2a** — development of a prediction model with external validation using separately collected data from the original dataset population. Development anchor: Colombian Aerospace Force (FAC) DIMAE 2010–2026 cohort. External validation: three previously published independent military-aviation cohorts (Morgagni 2010; Morgagni 2012; Landolfi 2009).

## Summary

All 22 TRIPOD 2015 items are addressed in the manuscript or in the accompanying open-source model card, with explicit page / section pointers above. Items 10e, 17 (model-updating) and 14b (unadjusted predictor-outcome associations) are not applicable to a mechanistic physics-informed model; these are reported as "Not done" or "Not applicable" with a rationale.

TRIPOD+AI (Collins 2024) does not apply: no machine-learning component is used in the deployed prediction pipeline (see §2.1 Study design and Declarations — Reporting guideline).
